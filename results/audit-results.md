### Executive Summary

*   Kaspi.kz operates a two-sided Super App business model, serving both consumers and merchants/entrepreneurs through the Kaspi.kz Super App and Kaspi Pay Super App.
*   The business is structured around three core platforms: Payments, Marketplace, and Fintech, which are integrated to offer a comprehensive suite of solutions.
*   The company's business model, supported by its brand and continuous product innovation, generates powerful network effects, contributing to growth across platforms and strong financial performance.
*   Kaspi Cloud LLC, established in 2022, provides critical data center services to other Group companies, supporting information storage, maintenance, and processing.
*   The Group designates investments in equity instruments at FVTOCI for long-term strategic reasons.
*   Technology & product development expenses have shown a consistent increase from 2022 to 2024, indicating ongoing investment in these areas.

### Key Risks / Bottlenecks

1.  **Dependency on the Super App Business Model:** The entire operational framework and offerings are built around the two-sided Super App model, making the business highly dependent on its continued success and adoption.
    Evidence: "Kaspi.kz operates a two-sided Super App business model: the Kaspi.kz Super App for consumers and the Kaspi Pay Super App for merchants and entrepreneurs."

2.  **Increasing Technology & Product Development Expenses:** While essential for innovation, the consistent increase in technology and product development expenses (from KZT 60,807 million in 2022 to KZT 109,553 million in 2024) represents a significant and growing cost center that requires efficient management and clear ROI.
    Evidence: "Technology & product development (60,807) (88,657) (109,553)"

3.  **Criticality of Kaspi Cloud LLC for Data Operations:** Kaspi Cloud LLC is responsible for providing data center services, including storage, maintenance, and processing of information, to other Group companies. Any disruption to these services could severely impact the Group's operations.
    Evidence: "Kaspi Cloud LLC is providing data center services to other companies of the Group, that support the storage, maintenance and processing of information using server software and equipment."

4.  **Reliance on Network Effects:** The business model's success is explicitly linked to "powerful network effects." A weakening of these effects could impede growth across platforms and impact financial performance.
    Evidence: "Our business model, reinforced by our highly recognizable brand and continuing product innovation, generates powerful network effects, which has resulted in growth across all our platforms and strong financial performance."

5.  **Exposure to Foreign Exchange Rate Risk:** The Group actively manages its exposure to foreign exchange rate risk through derivative financial instruments, indicating that this is a material financial risk.
