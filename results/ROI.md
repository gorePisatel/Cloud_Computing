## ROI.md

### ROI Framing
Value for Kaspi.kz is primarily defined by the growth and financial performance driven by its two-sided Super App business model, which leverages powerful network effects. This includes successful customer acquisition and high levels of customer engagement across its Payments, Marketplace, and Fintech platforms. Long-term strategic investments also contribute to value.

Evidence:
"Kaspi.kz operates a two-sided Super App business model: the Kaspi.kz Super App for consumers and the Kaspi Pay Super App for merchants and entrepreneurs. Our offerings include payments, marketplace and fintech solutions for both consumers and merchants."
"Our business model, reinforced by our highly recognizable brand and continuing product innovation, generates powerful network effects, which has resulted in growth across all our platforms and strong financial performance."
"Our Payments Platform is our main customer acquisition tool and we consider it to be fundamental for high levels of customer engagement."
"The Group has designated these investments in equity instruments at FVTOCI as the Group plans to hold them in the long term for strategic reasons."

### Measurement Plan
To measure the Return on Investment (ROI) for cloud computing initiatives, the following aspects should be considered:

**What to measure:**
- **Financial Performance:** This includes tracking key financial metrics such as net income before tax, and various operating expenses.
- **Platform Growth and Engagement:** Monitoring the growth across the Payments, Marketplace, and Fintech platforms, as well as customer acquisition and engagement levels.
- **Operational Efficiency:** Analyzing technology & product development expenses, transaction expenses, and cost of goods and services to identify efficiencies or reductions.
- **Data Processing and Storage Costs:** Specific costs related to data center services provided by Kaspi Cloud LLC.

**Data Sources:**
- **Consolidated Financial Statements:** These provide detailed financial figures for expenses and income over reporting periods.
- **Internal Operational Reports:** Data related to platform growth, customer acquisition, and engagement would be derived from internal operational metrics.
- **Kaspi Cloud LLC Internal Records:** Specific cost data related to data center services, storage, maintenance, and processing of information.
